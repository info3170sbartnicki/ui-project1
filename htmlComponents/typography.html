Cinzel Decorative
This is the primary typeface for our website, a serif style font inspired from first century roman inscriptions, and based on classical proportions.
Cinzel decorative will be the default typeface, a reflection of the overall brand identity, and will add an elegant feel and design to our brand.

Inter Tight | Secondary Font

Our secondary font is Inter Tight, this is the typeface that will be used for body text, forms, buttons, and all other elements. Below are the following headings for this font.
